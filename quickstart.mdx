  <Step title="Download and install the latest version">
    <Card title="MacOS Apple Silicon" icon="download" iconType="solid" horizontal href="https://download.getjumper.io">
      macOS Sonoma 14.0\+
    </Card>
    <Card title="MacOS Intel" icon="download" iconType="solid" horizontal href="https://getjumper.io/intel">
      macOS Sonoma 14.0\+
    </Card>
    <Card title="Windows" icon="download" iconType="solid" horizontal href="https://getjumper.io/windows">
      
    </Card>
    Requires Premiere `v24.0.0+`, Final Cut Pro `10.6.5+`, DaVinci Resolve `19.0.3+` or Avid Media Composer `2023.8+`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Jumper">
    When you first launch Jumper, you will be greeted with the following screen.

    ![SCR-20250509-ogwt.png](/images/SCR-20250509-ogwt.png)

    Here you can enable Jumper for the NLE of your choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the extension in your NLE">
    The method of opening the Jumper extension differ depending on the NLE.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Premiere Pro">
        Open the Jumper panel from the `Window > Extensions > Jumper` menu item
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Final Cut Pro">
        Open the Jumper panel from the `Window > Extensions > Jumper` menu item
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Davinci Resolve">
        Open the Jumper panel from the `Tools > Jumper` menu item
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Avid MC">
        Open the Jumper panel from the `Tools â†’ Jumper` menu item
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the onboarding">
    Follow the onboarding to setup the extension.

    <Info>
      You will need either a free trial license key or a paid license key to use Jumper.

      <CardGroup cols={2}>
        <Card title="Get a Free Trial key" horizontal="false" href="https://getjumper.io">
          Click the Start Free Trial button to get a license
        </Card>
        <Card title="Buy a License" horizontal="false" href="https://getjumper.io/#pricing">
          Available in Standard or Pro
        </Card>
      </CardGroup>
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze media">
    In the [Media tab](/interface/media-tab), mark your media for visual and/or speech analysis and click the `Process` button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search your media">
    Once the media is analyzed, you can start searching in the [Search tab](/interface/search-tab).

    Happy Jumping\!
  </Step>
</Steps>
