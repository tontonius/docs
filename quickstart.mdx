  <Step title="Launch Jumper">
    When you first launch Jumper, you will be greeted with the following screen.

    ![SCR-20250509-ogwt.png](/images/SCR-20250509-ogwt.png)

    Here you can enable Jumper for the NLE of your choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the extension in your NLE">
    The method of opening the Jumper extension differ depending on the NLE.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Premiere Pro">
        To open the Jumper extension, go to `Window → Extensions → Jumper`
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Final Cut Pro">
        To open the Jumper extension, go to Tools → Jumper
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Davinci Resolve">
        To open the Jumper extension, go to Tools → Jumper
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Avid MC">
        To open the Jumper extension, go to Tools → Jumper
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the onboarding">
    Follow the onboarding to setup the extension.

    <Info>
      You will need either a free trial license key or a paid license key to use Jumper.

      <CardGroup cols={2}>
        <Card title="Get a Free Trial key" horizontal>
          
        </Card>
        <Card title="Buy a License" horizontal>
          
        </Card>
      </CardGroup>
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze media">
    In the [Media tab](/interface/media-tab), mark your media for visual and/or speech analysis and click the `Process` button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search your media">
    Once the media is analyzed, you can start searching in the [Search tab](/interface/search-tab). 

    Happy Jumping\!
  </Step>
</Steps>
